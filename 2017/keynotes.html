Keynote Speakers

Experts from industry, academia, and government

New York University

Dr. Rumi Chunara

Dr. Chunara is an Assistant Professor in New York University's Computer Science & Engineering Department. The overarching goal of her research is to develop the principles needed to incorporate unstructured data into a better understanding of our health.

Continuum Analytics

Christine Doig

Christine Doig is a Senior Data Scientist at Continuum Analytics. She has over five years' experience in analytics, operations research and machine learning in a variety of industries, including energy, manufacturing and banking. At Continuum, she worked on MEMEX, a DARPA-funded project helping stop human trafficking. She is the co-author of Breaking Data Science Open, published by O'Reilly.

Cloudera

Juliet Hoagland

Juliet Hougland is Head of Data Science at Cloudera. She has years of experience with scalable, distributed data processing technology. In addition, Juliet has been a contributor in the open source community working on projects such as Apache Spark, Scalding, and Kiji.

Oak Ridge National Laboratory

Dr. Georgia Tourassi

Dr. Tourassi is Distinguished Staff scientist and Director of the Health Data Sciences Institute at Oak Ridge National Laboratory where she conducts research in biomedical informatics and clinical decision support systems.
